What We Offer

Our Products

From structural cement to construction aggregates — we have everything you need to build strong, lasting structures.

🏗️
Cement
PPC Cement
Portland Pozzolana Cement — ideal for residential construction, plastering, and masonry work. Offers high durability and resistance to moisture.
🧱
Cement
OPC 43 Grade Cement
Ordinary Portland Cement Grade 43 — the workhorse of construction. Best suited for general civil engineering works and pre-cast concrete products.
⭐
Cement · Premium
Extra Blended Premium Cement
High-performance blended cement for premium constructions. Enhanced strength, better workability, and longer service life.
💨
Aggregates
Dust (Reta)
Fine sand/dust used as a filler in concrete, plastering, and flooring applications. Sourced from quality riverbeds for consistent grain size.
🪨
Aggregates
Stone Dust
Crushed stone powder used in concrete mix, paver base, and as a binding agent. Provides stability and compressive strength to construction.
🔩
Aggregates · Gravel
Gravel 20mm
20mm crushed gravel for concrete production, pipe bedding, and drainage. Ideal for residential and light commercial slab work.
🪨
Aggregates · Gravel
Gravel 40mm
40mm gravel for road bases, sub-bases, and heavy-duty concrete work. Provides excellent load distribution for large structures.
⛏️
Aggregates · Gravel
Gravel 60mm
60mm coarse gravel for deep foundation work, retaining walls, and heavy civil engineering projects. Superior drainage properties.
